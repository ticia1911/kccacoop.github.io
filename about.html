About Us

KCCA Staff Multipurpose Cooperative Society

KCCA Staff Multipurpose Cooperative Society Limited is a member-owned financial institution founded by Kampala Capital City Authority (KCCA) staff to empower members through savings, affordable credit, and financial growth. Since our inception in 2013, we've grown from 398 to over 1,252 members. In 2021, we became a multipurpose cooperative to serve our members' evolving needs.

Our Vision

To become a preferred financial and investment partner that grows members.

Our Mission

To promote growth of members' welfare through savings and demand-driven financial solutions.

Our Values

Core Principles That Guide Us

Teamwork

We work cooperatively, involving others with care and valuing both individual and team contributions. We thrive in a workplace committed to empowering communities through high standards of innovation.

Professionalism

We care! We serve! We add value!

We are accessible and attentive to the interests of our members and diligently carry out our work for sustainability and mutual benefit.

Client Care

We are accountable and responsive to the interests and concerns of our members, carrying out our work diligently to ensure sustainability, transparency, and mutual benefit.

Integrity

We are a values-driven cooperative. Our annual principles emphasise honesty and transparency and accountability. Zero tolerance to corruption.

Inclusivity

We are accessible and responsive to the community and ensure that diversity and support can be successfully and mutual benefit.

Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards of service delivery in what we do, providing an exceptional member experience built on trust, efficiency, and a passion for excellence.
Our Purpose

Cooperative Objectives

KCCA Staff Multipurpose Cooperative Society Limited is committed to empowering our members through sustainable financial solutions and fostering a culture of savings and investment.

  • Develop savings culture & minimize financial risks
  • Provide affordable loans for member development
  • Support investment projects through training
  • Offer authorized financial services as needed
