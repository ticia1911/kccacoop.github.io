Toto Savings Account

A special young saver's account designed to help children and young people build their financial future with KCCA Cooperative.

  • Home
  • Savings
  • Toto Savings

This is a young saverâ€™s account. A member may open up a savings account for their children. It helps instill good saving habits from an early age while providing attractive returns and flexible access to funds throughout the year.

Features

  • Minimum savings amount of UGX 100,000
  • Permits 3 withdrawals in a year
  • Earns interest at end of year
  • Cannot be borrowed against
  • Designed specifically for young savers

Benefits

  • Teaches financial discipline from an early age
  • Annual interest earnings on savings
  • Limited withdrawals encourage long-term saving
  • Secure and regulated savings environment
  • Professional financial management
  • Foundation for future financial growth

Requirements

  • Parent/Guardian must be a KCCA staff member
  • Valid birth certificate or national ID
  • Completed Toto Savings application form
  • Initial deposit of UGX 100,000 minimum
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Parent/Guardian identification documents
