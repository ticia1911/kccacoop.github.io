Fixed Deposit Account

Secure your future with our high-yield fixed deposit accounts offering guaranteed returns and flexible terms.

  • Home
  • Savings
  • Fixed Deposits

KCCA Cooperative offers you an opportunity to invest in fixed deposits and earn guaranteed attractive returns. Our fixed deposit accounts provide higher yields with secure, time-based investments that guarantee your principal and interest earnings.

Features

  • Minimum deposit amount is UGX 1,000,000
  • Maximum fixing period is 12 months (one year)
  • Guaranteed interest rates based on selected term
  • Flexible term options: 3, 6, or 12 months
  • Principal and interest paid at maturity
  • Option to reinvest upon maturity

Interest Rates

  • 3 months – 9% per annum
  • 6 months – 10% per annum
  • 12 months – 11% per annum
  • Interest is calculated on daily balance
  • Interest is paid at maturity

Requirements

  • Must be a KCCA staff member
  • Valid national ID or passport
  • Completed fixed deposit application form
  • Minimum deposit of UGX 1,000,000
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Salary slip or employment letter
  • Signed fixed deposit agreement
