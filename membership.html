Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about membership

Membership applications are typically processed within 3-5 working days after submission of all required documents. You'll receive an SMS notification once your application is approved.

We require a minimum monthly savings contribution of UGX 50,000 for regular members. However, you're encouraged to save more to maximize your benefits and loan eligibility.

Regular savings can be withdrawn with 30 days notice. Fixed deposits have specific terms (6 months to 5 years) with higher interest rates. Emergency withdrawals may be approved under special circumstances.

Loan eligibility depends on:
1) Your savings balance (typically 3x the loan amount)
2) Length of membership (minimum 3 months for most loans)
3) Existing loan obligations
4) Your repayment capacity

Your membership remains active for 6 months after leaving KCCA. During this period, you can:
- Continue as an associate member (with different terms)
- Withdraw your savings
- Transfer to another SACCO
After 6 months, inactive accounts are subject to dormancy fees.
