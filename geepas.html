Geepas Asset Financing

The Cooperative management team is dedicated to finding the best financing solution tailored to your needs. Low interest rates, easy repayment terms- making it accessible for everyone.

  • Home
  • Products
  • GEEPAS Asset Financing

Applying for Asset Finance Loan is as easy as 1-2-3. Simply visit or drop by the Cooperative office and our friendly staff will guide you through the process.

Features

  • Eligibility: Must be a Cooperative member
  • Loan repayment capacity should be within 50% monthly contribution
  • Loans up to 4 million are repayable in 6 months
  • Loans above 4 million are repayable in 12 months
  • Comprehensive Insurance is mandatory for items costing 4 million and above
  • Interest rate is 14%

Procedure

  • A Cooperative member visits the showroom of GEEPAS on Jinja road, opposite Total to identify the item of their choice
  • GEEPAS issues quotation to Cooperative member
  • Member delivers quotation to Cooperative Credit Officer for credit screening
  • Once you pass the appraisal stage, an approval form is issued by the Accountant
  • Member will present this approval at the showroom to pick the asset
  • GEEPAS issues a delivery note to member to confirm release of asset
